Using your keyboard with "Quick Search..."

Once you are satisified with the results of your search - you can press the right arrow key on your keyboard to activate scrolling with the up and down arrows in the results. Press enter when you reach the result you want, and you will be taken to that page. To deactivate your keyboard - select the left arrow key.
